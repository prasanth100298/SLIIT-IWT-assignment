The duo behind JIFFY

Margot Robbie
Founder and CEO
Margot Robbie is the Founder & CEO of Jiffy, one of the world’s largest ride-sharing platforms and Sri Lanka’s most popular mobile app for transportation. With the mission of building mobility for a billion people, she started Jiffy (formerly FiffyCabs) in 2010 with Kozaksi Pazapugazh, a fellow alumnus from the Sri Lanka Institute of Technology in Colombo, the country’s premier institution for technology and science. Before Jiffy, Kozaksi worked with Microsoft Research for two years, filing two patents alongside having three papers published in international academic journals. True to her belief of the need to have on-demand consumer transportation as a service, Kozaksi doesn't own a car himself and vows that she never will!
Kozaksi Pazapugazh
Founder and CTO
Kozaksi is the Co-Founder & CTO of Jiffy, one of the world’s largest ride-sharing platforms and Sri Lanka’s most popular mobile app for transportation. With the mission of building mobility for a billion people, he started Jiffy (formerly JiffyCabs) in 2010 with Margot, a fellow alumnus from the Sri Lanka Institute of Technology in Colombo, the country’s premier institution for technology and science. At Jiffy, Kozaksi leads product, engineering, and data sciences, ensuring a strong back-end system that powers the experience of riders and driver partners alike. Scaling Jiffy in a short span of time, he has played a lead role in pioneering technologies such as mapping innovations, the world’s first connected car platform - Jiffy Play, and offline bookings that made Jiffy accessible for a large segment of the population.
